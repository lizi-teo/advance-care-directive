# Values Word List

Working draft for the values clarification feature. ~42 words curated for plain language, cultural inclusivity, and relevance to life and identity (not medically framed).

## Connection
Belonging, Closeness, Family, Friendship, Loyalty, Togetherness, Together

## Who I am
Being myself, Courage, Dignity, Gentleness, Humour, Independence, Integrity, Resilience, Strength

## How I live
Adventure, Creativity, Freedom, Joy, Playfulness, Simplicity, Stillness, Warmth

## What I stand for
Compassion, Faith, Goodness, Honesty, Justice, Kindness, Legacy, Truth, Wisdom

## What sustains me
Acceptance, Beauty, Community, Ease, Enough, Growth, Home, Nature, Peace, Security, Helping others, Spirituality

---

## Design principles
- Plain language throughout — if someone has to think about what a word means, the reflection is broken
- No medical or career framing
- No cultural or religious bias — words like Faith, Spirituality, Belonging, Together hold meaning across traditions
- Tension is intentional — e.g. Independence vs Togetherness, Adventure vs Stillness — so the elimination step means something
- Categories are for pacing the reveal in the UI, not necessarily labeled for the user

## Changes from original exercise (Values_LT.pdf)
- Removed career/status language: Achievement, Reputation, Market position, Supervising others, etc.
- Removed medically coded language
- Added: Acceptance, Ease, Enough, Dignity, Warmth, Home, Stillness, Being myself, Helping others
- Authenticity → Being myself
- Service → Helping others
- Removed: Uniqueness (overlaps with Being myself), Spontaneity (too formal), Presence (abstract standalone), Partnership (implies specific relationship type)
